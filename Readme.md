## 🚀 About Zentro

Zentro is a technology-driven platform that connects users with verified and trusted professionals on demand.

Whether you need a plumber, electrician, carpenter, architect, engineer, chartered accountant, interior designer, contractor, technician, consultant, or any other skilled professional, Zentro helps you find the right expert near you quickly, securely, and reliably.

Our mission is to eliminate the trust gap between customers and service providers through technology, verification, transparency, and intelligent matching.

---

## 🎯 Vision

To build the world's most trusted professional services network and become the default platform people use whenever they need skilled expertise.

---

## 🌎 Mission

Empower individuals and businesses by providing instant access to verified professionals while creating growth opportunities for skilled service providers.

---

## ❌ Problems We Are Solving

Finding reliable professionals remains a challenge due to:

- Lack of trust and verification
- Fake profiles and misleading information
- Delayed response times
- Poor service quality
- Inconsistent pricing
- Limited transparency
- No centralized professional network
- Difficulty finding nearby available experts

Users often rely on referrals, social media groups, or multiple platforms before finding the right professional.

---

## ✅ Our Solution

Zentro acts as a trusted bridge between customers and professionals.

### For Customers

- Discover nearby professionals
- Access verified profiles
- Compare ratings and reviews
- Book services instantly
- Track requests in real time
- Make secure payments
- Receive quality support

### For Professionals

- Create professional profiles
- Complete verification process
- Receive relevant service requests
- Manage bookings efficiently
- Build reputation through reviews
- Grow business through digital visibility
